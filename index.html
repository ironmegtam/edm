博彩要有節制
未滿十八歲人士不得投注或進入可投注的地方。
向非法或海外莊家下注，最高可被判監禁九個月及罰款三萬港元。
切勿沉迷賭博，如需尋求輔導協助，可致電平和基金熱線1834 633。


Play Responsibly
No person under 18 is allowed to bet or enter premises where bets are accepted.
The maximum penalty for betting with illegal or overseas bookmakers is 9 months' imprisonment and a HK$30,000 fine.
Don't gamble your life away. Call Ping Wo Fund hotline 1834 6333 if you need help or counselling.
